Amazon Prime vs. Walmart+: Which Subscription Wins in 2026?

An in-depth breakdown of pricing, delivery perks, streaming benefits, and retail store discounts.

ER
Elena Rostova
E-Commerce Expert • July 12, 2026 • 8 min read

As retail subscription prices evolve, shoppers are increasingly asking whether holding multiple memberships is worthwhile. Here is an honest, head-to-head evaluation of Amazon Prime and Walmart+.

Pricing & Core Value

Amazon Prime sits at $139/year or $14.99/month, while Walmart+ offers a lower price point at $98/year or $12.95/month. However, the value equation depends heavily on your local shopping habits.

Delivery & Grocery Advantage

Walmart+ leads in fresh grocery home delivery directly from local retail stores, whereas Amazon excels in nationwide two-day and same-day non-perishable merchandise fulfillment.
